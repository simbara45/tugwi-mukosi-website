Welcome to Tugwi Mukosi

Where culture meets excellence

Since our establishment in 2019, Tugwi Mukosi Secondary School has been committed to providing quality education while honoring our motto "Our Culture Our Heritage." We believe in nurturing well-rounded students who excel academically while staying connected to their roots.

Located along the Tugwi river, South Chivi district of Masvingo, we serve 437 students with dedicated teachers who maintain a 60:1 student-teacher ratio, ensuring every child receives the attention they deserve.
